Privacy Policy

Sherwood Exchange & the WOODIE app (sherwood.spot) · Last updated: 20 July 2026

Short version: Sherwood is a non-custodial interface to public blockchains. We don't require accounts, we never hold your funds or keys, we don't sell data, and we don't run ads. Most of what the app touches is public on-chain data by nature.

Who we are

Sherwood Exchange ("Sherwood", "we") provides a web and mobile interface for trading tokens on Robinhood Chain, including the WOODIE copilot. The Android app is a wrapper around the website at sherwood.spot. Contact: anggarasepta88@gmail.com.

What we collect

  • No account data by default. You can use Sherwood by connecting a wallet; we don't ask for your name, phone number, or address.
  • Wallet addresses & transactions. These are public blockchain data. Our points/leaderboard service indexes public on-chain activity (addresses, transaction hashes, amounts) — nothing that isn't already on the public ledger.
  • Optional email login. If you sign in with email, authentication and the embedded wallet are provided by Privy. Your email is processed by Privy under Privy's privacy policy; we receive an identifier and your wallet address, not your password.
  • WOODIE chat messages. Messages you send to the WOODIE copilot are processed by our server and a third-party large-language-model provider to generate replies. Don't put sensitive personal data in chat. Chat history is kept locally in your browser, not in an account profile on our servers.
  • Standard server logs. Our servers and proxies keep short-lived technical logs (IP address, user agent, request path) for security and abuse prevention.
  • Local storage. Preferences, watchlists, cached market data, and (if you shield funds) encrypted note data live in your browser's local storage on your device. We can't read them.

What we don't do

  • We never custody funds or private keys. Transactions are signed by your wallet.
  • We don't sell or rent personal data.
  • We don't serve third-party advertising or ad trackers.

Third-party services

The app talks to these services to function; each processes only what's needed for its job:

  • Privy — optional email/social login and embedded wallets.
  • Reown (WalletConnect) — connecting external wallets.
  • DexScreener & GeckoTerminal — public market data (prices, charts, trades).
  • Blockchain RPC nodes and our relayer — reading the chain and broadcasting the transactions you sign. Shielded-pool transactions are relayed so your public wallet isn't linked on-chain; the relayer sees only the public legs of those transactions.
  • LLM inference provider — generating WOODIE chat replies.

Data retention

Technical logs are rotated on a short schedule. Indexed on-chain data mirrors the public blockchain and persists as long as the service runs. Anything in your browser's local storage is yours to clear at any time (browser settings → clear site data).

Your choices

  • Use a self-custody wallet instead of email login — then no email is ever involved.
  • Clear local storage to remove chat history, watchlists, and cached data.
  • Email us to ask about or request deletion of any off-chain data tied to your address (note: we cannot delete anything from a public blockchain).

Children

Sherwood is not directed at children and is not intended for anyone under 18.

Not financial advice

Information in the app, including WOODIE's replies, is provided for informational purposes and is not investment advice. Crypto assets and tokenized stocks are volatile; trade at your own risk.
